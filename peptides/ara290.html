1 What It Does

ARA-290 (cibinetide) is an 11-amino-acid peptide derived from erythropoietin's tissue-protective domain. It targets the innate repair receptor — studied for neuropathic pain, nerve repair, and chronic inflammatory conditions.

+Innate repair receptor +Neuropathic pain relief +Nerve fiber repair +Anti-inflammatory +Small-fiber neuropathy
