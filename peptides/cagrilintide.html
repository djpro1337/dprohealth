Amylin analog — appetite suppression.

1 What It Does

Cagrilintide is a long-acting amylin analog that promotes satiety and slows gastric emptying — suppressing appetite through a different pathway than GLP-1s. It's often paired with semaglutide (as CagriSema) for additive weight-loss effect.

+Amylin receptor agonist +Appetite suppression +Slows gastric emptying +Complements GLP-1s +Long-acting

GLP-1 complement

Cagrilintide works through amylin, not GLP-1 — which is why it stacks well with semaglutide/tirzepatide for appetite control via two pathways.
